About Us

Stubborn Squanderers are individuals who are: Driven by Curiosity; Ambitious, Spunky & Persistent; Romances with Risks at all times; Resilient to ride long distances and Student of Life

Avantika Singh

Avantika Singh received her B.Tech. in Electronics & Communication Engineering from Birla Institute of Technology, Mesra, India in 2014 and M.Sc. in Integrated Circuit Design, jointly offered by Nanyang Technological University, Singapore and Technical University of Munich, Germany in 2016, while, serving as a Student Mitarbeiter at Fraunhofer IIS, Erlangen. From 2017 to 2019, she worked with Semtech Ltd. UK as an R&D Analogue IC Design Engineer on their optical transceiver IC products.

She has been the recipient of 2020 IEEE SSCS Rising Star Award. She is currently working towards her PhD (Fall 2020 onwards) with a focus on mixed-signal/RF IC design in conjunction with system level signal processing algorithms.

Harshit Naman

Harshit Naman received his B.Tech. in Electronics & Communication Engineering and In-Depth Specialization in VLSI design from Birla Institute of Technology, Mesra, India in 2022. He worked as an Undergraduate Research Intern at R.C.I Lab, D.R.D.O Hyderabad, designing and implementing circuits for MEMS Gyroscopes. He completed his undergraduate thesis at the Indian Institute of Technology Hyderabad in 2022.


He will be joining Purdue University, West Lafayette, in Fall 2022 for pursuing his MS(VLSI and Circuit design) and PhD with a focus on Analog/RF integrated circuit design.
